A. Jesse Jiryu Davis is a portrait photographer based in New York City, trained at the International Center of Photography.

Jesse has covered events, created editorial portraits, and photographed landscapes for New York Magazine, The Village Voice, Time Out New York, Meditate NYC, AT Magazine in Germany, and the Village Zendo Journal. Currently, he is documenting the New York Zen community and the Lower East Side.

In past lives, Jesse earned a BA in Computer Science from Oberlin College, and trained for a year in a Zen monastery.
